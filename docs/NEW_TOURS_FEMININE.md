# New Tours: Design & Style Collection

These tours target the Souk & Co audience at a private (not group) price point.

---

## NEW TOUR 1: Souks & Style Escape
**5 Days | Marrakech-based | Design + Shopping Focus**

*For the woman who travels for beauty, not bucket lists.*

### Overview
Five days immersed in Marrakech's most beautiful spaces. Boutique riads. Artisan workshops. Hidden courtyards. The souks with someone who knows which doors to knock on.

### Day by Day

**Day 1: Arrival**
- Airport pickup
- Settle into your riad (Riad Yasmine-style, tiled courtyard, rooftop terrace)
- Evening walk through the medina as the light turns gold
- Dinner in a candlelit courtyard

**Day 2: Medina Immersion**
- Rooftop breakfast overlooking the rooftops
- Morning with a local guide through the souks—leather, brass, spices, textiles
- Lunch at a hidden restaurant behind an unmarked door
- Afternoon: Bahia Palace (the tilework alone is worth the trip)
- Optional hammam experience

**Day 3: Atlas Escape**
- Drive into the foothills to Kasbah Bab Ourika
- Lunch overlooking terraced valleys
- Visit a women's cooperative (argan, saffron, or textiles)
- Return to Marrakech via the rose water distilleries
- Evening free to wander or rest

**Day 4: Design Day**
- Morning: Majorelle Garden (arrive early, before the crowds)
- Yves Saint Laurent Museum
- Lunch at a garden restaurant
- Afternoon: Private visit to artisan workshops (zellige tile makers, brass workers)
- Final souk run—you know what you want now
- Farewell dinner on a rooftop terrace

**Day 5: Departure**
- Breakfast at your own pace
- Airport transfer
- Fly home with a suitcase full of treasures

### Price: €590 per person (2 pax) / €490 (4 pax)
### Includes: Private driver, 4 nights boutique riad, all breakfasts, 2 dinners, guide for souk day, artisan workshop visits

## NEW TOUR 2: Desert & Design
**7 Days | Marrakech → Sahara → Marrakech**

*The full experience. Medina magic and desert silence.*

### Overview
Start in the pink city. End in the pink city. In between: rose valleys, ancient kasbahs, and a night under more stars than you knew existed. This is the Morocco trip you've been pinning for years.

### Day by Day

**Day 1: Marrakech**
- Arrive and settle into your riad
- Evening medina walk
- Welcome dinner

**Day 2: Over the Atlas**
- Tizi n'Tichka pass (stop for photographs and mint tea)
- Ait Benhaddou—the kasbah from every film
- Lunch in Ouarzazate
- Continue to Dades Valley
- Night in a kasbah above the river

**Day 3: Gorges & Dunes**
- Morning walk in Todra Gorge (the walls are 300 meters high)
- Through date palm oases and pink rock
- Arrive at Merzouga
- Camel ride into the dunes at sunset
- Dinner and drums by firelight
- Sleep in a desert camp

**Day 4: Desert Morning**
- Sunrise over the dunes
- Breakfast at camp
- Option: 4x4 to nomad families, Gnawa music village
- Lunch at the edge of the sand
- Begin return journey—different route through Ziz Valley
- Night in Midelt

**Day 5: Cedar Forests**
- Drive through Middle Atlas
- Stop at Azrou—Barbary macaques in ancient cedars
- Ifrane (the "Switzerland of Morocco")
- Arrive Marrakech late afternoon
- Evening free

**Day 6: Marrakech Deep Dive**
- Guided souk experience
- Artisan workshops
- Lunch hidden in the medina
- Afternoon at leisure—hammam, shopping, or simply tea on your terrace
- Farewell dinner

**Day 7: Departure**
- Breakfast
- Transfer to airport

### Price: €890 per person (2 pax) / €690 (4 pax)
### Includes: Private 4x4, 6 nights accommodation (3 boutique riads, 2 kasbahs, 1 desert camp), all breakfasts, 4 dinners, camel trek, guide in Marrakech

## NEW TOUR 3: Agafay & Marrakech
**3 Days | No long drives | Glamping + Medina**

*For the time-poor who still want the magic.*

### Overview
You don't have time for the Sahara. That's okay. The Agafay desert is 45 minutes from Marrakech—rocky moonscape, not sand, but the same sunset gold and silence. Combine with two nights in the medina and you'll wonder why you ever thought you needed more.

### Day by Day

**Day 1: Marrakech**
- Arrive and settle in
- Afternoon medina wander with a guide
- Dinner on a rooftop as the call to prayer echoes

**Day 2: Agafay**
- Late breakfast
- Drive to Agafay Desert (45 mins)
- Lunch at a lodge overlooking the desert
- Afternoon at leisure—pool, walk, read, nothing
- Sunset drinks as the light turns pink then gold then purple
- Dinner under the stars
- Sleep in a luxury tent

**Day 3: Return & Depart**
- Sunrise over the rocky desert
- Breakfast
- Optional camel ride or 4x4 excursion
- Return to Marrakech
- Transfer to airport (or extend your stay)

### Price: €390 per person (2 pax)
### Includes: Private driver, 1 night medina riad, 1 night Agafay luxury camp, all meals at camp, guide for medina walk
